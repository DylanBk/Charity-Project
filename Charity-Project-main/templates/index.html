Mental Health Matters

If you're a young person, a parent, or work with young people, YoungMinds is here to help. YoungMinds has been helping young people with their mental health since 1993 and intends to keep providing support. Mental health issues are increasing yearly, so awareness and support matter. A single donation can make a significant difference to young people's health, or you can choose to donate monthly and help improve their lives. If you cannot donate, then you can spread awareness of mental health by sharing this or the official site with your friends and family.
